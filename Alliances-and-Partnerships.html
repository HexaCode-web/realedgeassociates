Alliances and Partnerships

Home  >   Alliances and Partnerships
We partner with other organisations to:
  1. enhance and complement each partner’s individual strengths to provide clients with a more complete service,
  2. increase client knowledge between partners to help understand the industry and
  3. increase combined market potential of each partner

Partnerships

Global Partners collaborate to develop and offer data science offerings to mutual clients and prospects. These partners are normally sector focused, innovation driven and have a naturally high symbiotic relationship with REA, a machine learning specialist.

Technology Partners provide complementary technologies to REA’s services. These partners enhance REA’s data science services and include data capture tools, visualization tools, hardware and software solutions.

Service Providers Are Agencies and Marketing Services companies who work with REA to target the right customers via digital interactions. What’s of utmost importance is to optimize marketing budgets, construct an appropriate test and learn environment and predict outcomes accurately.

Solution Providers use REA to provide “behind-the-scenes” data science services to current or new clients. Typically, REA delivers a core machine learning solution that is key in the Solution Provider’s business offering.
