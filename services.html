REA Service Models

In most cases a data science product is a unique solution to address a specific problem. REA offer a range of service models depending on what the client wants. For very specific services REA offer a competitive consultancy model allowing you to access global specialists.

  • Consultancy
    • Data Science Governance, Policy and Frameworks.
    • Big or bespoke data capture, organisation and storage processing.
    • Machine learning Consultancy.
  • Product Development
    • Data Science Platform Development

Consultancy

While we will be introduced as external consultants our delivery is contingent on blending a deep understanding of your domain with our knowledge of data science. This will start the process of balancing tactical and strategic goals to deliver value from your data. Clients will normally seeks a broad or acute objective:

  • Specific intelligence, normally required on a regular frequency to optimize business processes.
  • General analysis, pattern extractions and verification or challenges to prior assumptions.

REA will service both requirements based on either tactical or strategic objectives. Along the way specific streams of consultancy will emerge.

Data Science Governance

For many it is well accepted that data science will form a fundamental component in the organisation. Often the question is how one should approach this challenge to makes sure value is achieved at each stage of the investment process.

Governance is the establishment of policies, and continuous monitoring of their proper implementation, by the members of the organization. We will set up the right organizational structures to maximize the value of your data and ensure ongoing benefit. Further, our bespoke software solutions, PMetric, will help to make management of processes automated and simple.

Data Science Infrastructure

Data Science Infrastructure includes software and hardware that can be grouped into the following categories:

  • Data Storage and Extraction
  • Data Science Research Environments
  • Data Science Product Platforms

Our experts will design and implement a high-performance, integrated platform or components to support a strategic architecture for Data Science.

Algorithmic Development

At the core of the Data Science product is the development of algorithms that can provide valuable decision information. REA will:

  • Data science problems are complex and individual. Proper solutions require specific research and development, and are more than likely to be unique.
  • It is normal through the research that wider ancillary insight is extracted from the domain providing clients with rich new information.
  • The algorithms we deliver for your problem will be unique to you, meaning you have unique IP in your organisation.

Product development

Product development is the process of designing, creating and marketing new products or services to benefit customers. A discipline in itself, it is focused on developing systematic methods for guiding all the processes involved in getting a new product to market.

Product development involves either improving an existing product or its presentation or developing a new product to target a particular market segment or segments.

Consistent product development is a necessity for companies striving to keep up with changes and trends in the marketplace to ensure their future profitability and success.

We are serious about product development. It is the final process that delivers the end value to your organisation. Our goal is to produce high quality professionally managed products that you will trust and depend on.

We have developed products for pricing in financial markets, business productivity, and healthcare service optimization and news filtering. As a cross vertical team we can deliver across different sectors and organisation types.

Clients chose REA for product development because:

  • We offer a complete service across the full product lifecycle. That means we can design, deliver and support mission critical enterprise software solutions.
  • We are Big Data ready meaning we can handle both large and exotic data sets and operate in high performance environments.
  • We follow strict procedures to ensure data security and solution risk management is central to our process.
  • We can integrate sophisticated machine learning technology.
  • We have a track of delivering enterprise Data Science solutions for large organisations.
