Mission Statement

The REA mission is to provide governance, framework, software technology and data science technology to maximise the value of any data set.

In particular we :

  • Work with Industries to define objective intelligence (decision information) for a data set.
  • Drive research into real world high value challenges based on these objectives.
  • Delivering commercial Machine Learning solutions.

Company Overview

REA is a consulting and product development company specializing in machine learning. Its founders, former hedge fund managers, chief technologists and academics from leading global organizations, have combined to provide cutting edge solutions for global organisations.

Information has always been an advantage. As companies mature to capture larger and more complex data sets REA offer the ability to unlock information advantage. REA has developed the expertise to translate new ideas into high value real world products, offering information driven innovation.

While for a relatively young field much real progress has been made, experts acknowledge that there are still large challenges in ensuring machine learning meets the expectation of industry challenges. For that reason, REA, principled in academic thinking, believe the correct approach to applying machine learning is based on incremental thoughtful steps. A thorough understanding of the client and domain is key to providing effective commercial value through data science.
